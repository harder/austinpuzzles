---
title: About The Austin Puzzle Exchange
summary: A free, neighborhood-driven puzzle swap network across Austin.
updated: "2026-02-13"
---
The Austin Puzzle Exchange helps neighbors trade jigsaw puzzles for free. It started as a family idea and grew into a shared community resource inspired by Little Free Libraries.

You can borrow puzzles, complete them at your own pace, and return them for the next puzzler.
